Ambrosia cupcakes

Ingredients

  • Blue food dye
  • Butter
  • Chocolate chips
  • Popcorn
  • Sugar

Method

  1. Make the chocolate chip cupcake base and add blue dye
  2. Make italian meringue butter cream
  3. Dye half of the buttercream blue
  4. Cover half the cupcake with white buttercream and cover with chopped popcorn
  5. Cover the other half with blue buttercream so the cupcake looks like a see shore
