Snow white's apple pie

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Sugar cookie dough

Method

  1. Roll out the sugar cookie dough and place it in a buttered pie pan
  2. Wash your apples do not peal them
  3. Take the core of the apple out
  4. Cut them in half and then in thin slices across
  5. Place all the apple slices in boiling water for a minute
  6. Starting with the outside and the bigger slices place your apple slices along the edge with each slice slightly overlaping
  7. Bake until your dough is golden
